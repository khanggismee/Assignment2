Project Ideas

At this time when the pandemic is still going on, staying home is the only option for people, especially in Vietnam. However, staying home has caused multiple problems, both physically and mentally. People can deal with physical problems like doing exercise, but not everyone knows how to deal with their mental issues. My project idea is creating an application that can help gamers get better at their favorite games. Not all people are born gamers and not all gamers are naturally good at the game that they love. Next, they are asked to choose their preferred games. Then the application will show information about the games that are chosen, both descriptions and how the games work. The application will display the gamer’s ingame account information, such as levels, experiences and ranks. It will also gather information about old gameplays from that account and generate statistics on provided qualities. For example, you created an account, and you chose Valorant as your favorite game. You typed in your ingame name, and the application will show your KDA (Kills. Deaths, Assists), your headshot percentages, your ACEs, etc. 

 

The application would also recommend ways to improve your skills, for example: To improve your aim, you can download AIMLAB. There will also be advertisements relevant to gaming and specifically to your chosen games. When you go into the rift, there will be timers for buffs and recommended skills. The application will show if you are over performing and underperforming. You can also add your friends’ accounts to compare your ranks and performance to see who is better. The application also allows chats and calls within your friend list; therefore you can communicate with your friend with only one application open. There are several applications that are similar to Project X, such as Blitz or FaceCheck. However, Project X is different from them because the overlay will work smoothly without causing lag. Moreover, if gamers spend a lot of time on their favorite games, they will get ingame money for the time that they spent. For example, you spend two hours playing Valorant, after that you will earn 10 VP for two hours that you spent. Therefore, gamers can save up money and buy ingame items which also encourage them to play more. The application supports PC and Mobile, Windows and MacOS, IOS and Android OS and gamers can download it for free. It supports Windows 8 and above, the newest MacOS, IOS 14 and Android 11.

For the user interface (UI) of the application, I will use JavaScript and Adobe XD to create it. For the user experience (UX), I will use both Python and Adobe XD to create this part. As for hardware, having a server is an important factor. Therefore, I will be purchasing a server that can contain up to 2000 accounts. IT Technologies

The two areas that I chose are: cloud computing and blockchain and cryptocurrencies.

“Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services - including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and intelligence - over the Internet (“the cloud”) to offer faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.” (Microsoft) There are three types of cloud computing: public cloud, private cloud and hybrid cloud. Third-party cloud service providers own and operate public clouds, and deliver their computing resources over the Internet, such as servers and storage. A private cloud, straightforward, is private cloud computing resources that are owned and used by a business or organization exclusively. It can be located on the organization or company’s data center. Some companies would pay third-party service providers to host their own private cloud. Private cloud also comes with a private network so that all the data can be protected. Hybrid cloud is a combination of private cloud and public cloud. Service providers bound these two clouds with their technology and allow applications and data to be shared between those two. Hybrid cloud gives the business greater flexibility and can help optimize the system by allowing data and applications to move between public and private clouds. Now let’s talk about the types of cloud services. There are four types of cloud services: IaaS, PaaS, serverless, and SaaS. Infrastructure as a service (IaaS) is the most basic category of cloud computing services. You can rent servers, storage, etc. via IaaS from a cloud provider and pay as you use the service. Platform as a service (PaaS) is a cloud computing service that supplies an on-demand environment for developing, testing, delivering, and managing software applications. Developers use PaaS to create web or mobile applications quickly and they do not have to worry about setting up or managing the servers. Serverless computing focuses on building applications functionality without spending time continually managing the servers and infrastructure required to do so. You are given the setup, capacity planning, and server management from the cloud provider. Serverless computing is highly scalable and object-oriented. Software as a service (SaaS) delivers software applications over the Internet, on demand and on a subscription basis. With SaaS, you are given the maintenance of the application, such as upgrades and security patches, while the cloud providers host and manage the infrastructure and the application itself.  In general, cloud computing provides a variety of computing services so that clients can choose which type of cloud computing they want to use. Now let’s talk about the impact of cloud computing. “Cloud computing in not only the future of computing but the present and entire past of computing”, - Larry Ellison. The impact of cloud computing can be divided into two main categories: business and workforce. On the business side, cloud computing provides a method for companies to store their resources online. Companies can access their resources very easily anytime and anywhere. Because resources are getting bigger, more and more companies are moving towards cloud computing. It helps companies to reduce the cost of accessing resources. Since cloud computing works on the concept of pay-per-use, companies get access to their resources only when they need to. Scalability is a big factor for companies. With cloud computing, they can scale up when the resources increase and scale down when the resources decrease. Moreover, they can do it at any time they want. Flexibility is also an important key point for choosing cloud computing. Companies can choose what type of cloud computing that they think would fit their business, based on their needs of security and other factors. The most important factor about cloud computing is storage. Cloud computing provides almost unlimited storage; therefore companies do not have to worry about the amount of storage to store their data. For the workforce, cloud computing has created tons of opportunities for organizations. It creates a virtual workspace which can be accessed by employees anywhere in the world, making it easier for the employees and increasing their productivity. However, because of the lack of awareness as well as the fear of losing a job in the IT industry, cloud computing was accepted a bit slow. Overall, cloud computing has been changing the world economically. This leads to changes in other fields as well. Cloud computing has been changing the entertainment industry, bringing people more and more content. Examples of entertaining cloud-based applications are YouTube, Spotify, Netflix, etc. Shopping has always been everyone’s favorite aspect, now it is made easier and more convenient by cloud computing. Online shopping has become a trend as Amazon, AliExpress, etc. allows people to order and shop online without having to go to the mall or supermarket. In this time of Covid-19, health care is the most important factor. Cloud computing allows doctors to track numbers of patients, vaccines and the overall trend of this pandemic. It also supports storing patients records and other important health databases. Education also benefits from cloud computing, as universities now offer online courses. Students can access the material and data at anytime and anywhere. 

“A blockchain is a decentralized ledger of all transactions across a peer-to-peer network.” (pwc.com). With blockchain, users can confirm transactions without a central clearing authority. Applications that use this technology can include trading, voting, funding, etc. Here is how blockchain works. When someone requests a transaction, the requested transaction is reported to a P2P network consisting of computers, known as nodes. Using algorithms, the network of nodes validates the transaction and the user status. Once verified, the transaction is combined with other transactions to create a new block of data for the ledger. After the transaction, the new block is added to the existing blockchain, in a way that is permanent and unchangeable. The transaction is then completed. Some of blockchain's potential applications are automotive, financial services, voting and healthcare. For automotive application, consumers can use blockchain to manage fractional ownership in autonomous vehicles. For financial services, the technology can save billions of dollars from transaction costs by creating faster and cheaper settlements while improving transparency. Voting application uses a blockchain code, which allows voters to cast their vote via smartphones, tablets or computers, and then return the verifiable results immediately. For healthcare, patients’ health information is encrypted and could be shared with multiple providers without the risk of privacy breaches. Now let’s talk about cryptocurrency. “Cryptocurrency is a medium of exchange, created and stored electronically in the blockchain, using encryption techniques to control the creation of monetary units and to verify the transfer of funds. The first and best example of cryptocurrencies is Bitcoin, which was created by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008. Cryptocurrency has no intrinsic value. In other words, it is not redeemable for another commodity, such as diamonds and gold. Cryptocurrency is a virtual currency; therefore it has no physical form and exists only on the internet. No bank can supply cryptocurrency and the network is completely decentralized. The total value of all cryptocurrencies is 2.2 trillion dollars, and it comes in a variety of types, such as Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, etc. Cryptocurrencies are becoming more and more popular for some reasons. Supporters think that cryptocurrencies will be the currency of the future and many people do not like the idea of banking, which explains why they support cryptocurrency and are rushing to buy them. Some people just love the technology behind these virtual encrypted coins and because it does not need any banking system, the process of transaction is much more secure than traditional payment systems. Now let’s talk about the impact of blockchain and cryptocurrencies. Many banks are already using blockchain technology to improve the transactions because it allows money to be exchanged faster, more efficiently and more securely. Hacking has always been a threat to cybersecurity and using the blockchain technology can help secure the data. By verifying and encrypting data, hackers are not likely to hack into the system because the servers are centralized, and they are very susceptible to hacking. Blockchain also helps reduce data loss and errors. For healthcare, doctors do not have to worry about looking for a patient record when all of the data is stored on one single central database. Moreover, hospitals can store medical and vaccine records and only doctors and nurses are able to access it. What about us? How will blockchain and cryptocurrencies affect our life? First of all, they will shape the perception of people about what money actually is. Secondly, they make everything convenient for us. We can get access to our money anytime and anywhere, our transactions would be much faster and efficient, and our information and records are all in one database so that we do not have to worry about losing our identities.
